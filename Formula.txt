Formula for text-as-data research 


What question do you want to answer? This should be something you *actually* want to know, and it should end in a question mark.

How has Antonio Brown's tweets have changed prior to getting a concussion (hit by Burfict) and after?

What source of text data will you use?
I will be using Antonio Brown's tweets.

What are two possible answers to the question?
Two possible answers are there is or isn't a significant difference between how he was tweeting.

How will the results of the quantitative text analysis demonstrate which of those answers is correct?
The results of the quantitative text analysis will show how many different type of unique words were used before and after the concussion.
